Common Questions

How do I apply for a scholarship?

Click on any scholarship card and select "Apply Now". Fill out the application form with your details and submit.

Can I edit my application after submitting?

Applications cannot be edited after submission. Please review all information before submitting.

How long does it take to review applications?

Review times vary by scholarship. Check the scholarship details for estimated review timelines.

What if I don't meet the requirements?

You can still apply! Some scholarships may have flexible requirements. Contact the scholarship provider for more information.
