Frequently Asked Questions

How does the scholarship matching work?

Our algorithm analyzes your profile, academic achievements, interests, and background to match you with scholarships where you meet the eligibility criteria.

Is there a cost to use ScholarSeek?

No, ScholarSeek is completely free for students. We're committed to making education accessible to everyone.

How do I track my application status?

Once you've applied, you can monitor the status of each application in your personal dashboard, which updates in real-time.

Can I apply to multiple scholarships at once?

Yes! Our one-click application feature allows you to apply to multiple scholarships simultaneously using your pre-filled information.
